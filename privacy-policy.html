DryRoute – Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 9, 2026

DryRoute ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and your rights as a user.

1. Data We Collect

Location

DryRoute requests access to your device's location to fetch local weather and radar data. Location is used only while the app is in use (foreground) or when a background refresh is triggered. We do not store your location on any server — it is sent directly to the weather API (Open-Meteo) and is not retained.

Health Data (Watch app only)

The Apple Watch companion app optionally reads and writes workout data (heart rate, active energy, distance) to Apple Health via HealthKit. This data never leaves your device or Apple's Health infrastructure. We do not access, store, or share your health data.

Usage Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics (Google) to collect anonymised usage data such as which features are used and crash reports. This helps us improve the app. Analytics can be disabled at any time in Settings → Privacy inside the app. No personally identifiable information is collected.

Advertising

Free users see banner advertisements served by Google AdMob. AdMob may use your device's advertising identifier (IDFA) to show relevant ads. You will be asked for permission to track before any advertising identifier is accessed (App Tracking Transparency). You can opt out at any time in your device's Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any account information.
  • We do not require you to create an account.
  • We do not sell your data to third parties.
  • We do not store your location on our servers.

3. Third-Party Services

  • Open-Meteo – weather data (open-source, no personal data sent beyond coordinates). Privacy policy
  • Buienradar – minute-level rain forecast for the Netherlands. Coordinates only.
  • Firebase Analytics – anonymised usage analytics by Google. Privacy policy
  • Google AdMob – advertising (free users only). Privacy policy

4. Data Retention

Analytics data is retained by Firebase for up to 14 months, after which it is automatically deleted. All other app data (favorites, settings) is stored locally on your device and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

5. Children's Privacy

DryRoute is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Your Rights

You can opt out of analytics at any time inside the app (Settings → Privacy → Analytics). You can reset or limit your advertising identifier in your device's system settings. You may request deletion of any analytics data by contacting us.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will always reflect the most recent revision. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
